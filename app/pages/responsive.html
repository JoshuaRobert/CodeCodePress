media

media queries for most devices.

Either use numbers:
@include media(200px, max)
outputs as
@media screen and (max-width: 200px)

scopes are:
max: from number to smaller
min: until number

Or use proper device names: ipad, iphone6, iphone5, iphone4 (more to come)
scopes are:
strict: apply only to device
landscape: apply to landscape or smaller
portrait: apply to portrait or smaller
